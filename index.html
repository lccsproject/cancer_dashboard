One In Greater Detail - Cancer Registry

The NCRI provides interactive dashboards and high-quality datasets on cancer trends. In 2021, over 25,000 cases were reported. The site aims to visualize trends, explore risks, and inform the public about prevention and treatment using comprehensive datasets.

Plan and Design

A top-down, modular approach is used. The site includes graphs, filters, and user surveys. Data is visualized using charts and analyzed with Python. Feedback will be collected via surveys on public awareness and preferences for cancer care.

Objectives

  • Improve public understanding of cancer and its societal impact
  • Learn about historical trends and preventive strategies
