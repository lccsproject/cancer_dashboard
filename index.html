One In Greater Detail - National Cancer Registry Ireland

The National Cancer Registry Ireland provides an interactive dashboard analyzing cancer trends in Ireland focusing on diagnosis, morality, median age at diagnosis and death. Correlations between each topic are visualized using bar charts, line charts and tables. The National Cancer Registry Ireland has published its “Cancer in Ireland 1994-2022: Annual Statistical Report” which is one of the most trusted sources of information on cancer in Ireland. In 2021 25,331 people were diagnosed with invasive cancer 13,425 of which were male and 11906 were female. The risk of getting cancer being a male was 1 in 3 and most commonly diagnosed cancer that year was prostate. Whereas for females the risk was 1 in 4 and the most common type of cancer was breast coming in a 3802 being diagnosed. Additional data from Irish Cancer Society and International Agency for Research on Cancer (IARC). A weakness is the time lag on data availability, as the last report they sent out was 1994-2021, this is because of the time required for validation and analysis. An advantage of this is that it is the data is comprehensive and high-quality.  My Personal Interest is because of scientific curiosity, I am interested in analysing cancer trends as I can then understand risk factors and the environment and economic impact cancer has on our lives. Plan and Design

The solution I will address is the role of cancer and its impact on Ireland, focusing on Mortality, Types of Cancers, and the Risk of each type of cancer I am addressing. I will create an interactive website with clickable graphs, and line charts to explore data, visualising trends and show cancers and the impact and how important public knowledge is. The site will include a user survey to gather feedback on Cancer and provide recommendations for treatment and preventions.

meeting requirements

  • To collect and prepare the data, I will select datasets from reliable sources such as the NCRI, IARC & EEA. Using Python Pandas, I will extract, clean, and format the data, ensuring it is ready for analysis and export it to .csv files. For data analytics and visualization, I will use Python Pandas DataFrames to analyze the dataset and Matplotlib to create bar charts and line graphs highlighting cancer types and the trends of each one - Visualization will have titles, axis labels, and legends.
  • The interactive information system will be a web-based dashboard displaying key information and data. To ensure users can interact with visualizations, I will add features like hovering for details, clicking to filter data by region or year, and adjusting ranges for trend analysis. A user survey will collect data on trends and public awareness of cancer types using JavaScript for 2 types of data (cancer awareness perception and healthcare and screening preferences). Data will be stored in a database or file, summarized, and displayed to show overall trends.
